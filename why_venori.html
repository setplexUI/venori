Why Venori?

Our only goal is to ensure that you have the best possible experience while looking for that perfect and sparkly piece of jewelry, at the lowest price.

We are nothing without you!

We are here for your every beck and call. So if you're in need of assistance, have a question, or need advice about one of our pieces, please feel free to call, email, or chat with us. We'd love to hear from you!

We perform thorough inspections to achieve perfection

All of our pieces are inspected by 10 different sets of eyes before it gets to you. You'll recieve it only if it's perfect. We pinky promise.

More sparkle for less

We don't believe in high markups. So that means you can rest assured that you are getting the best bang for your buck when you shop with us. If you happen to find a similar stone with similar specs for less, we will match it and beat it by a dollar. Click here for more information.
